What information does the Application collect and how is it used?

Local Diary Data:
Since this is a personal diary app, all your diary entries and data are stored locally on your device only. The app does not provide any account creation or sign-in functionality, and we do not have access to your diary entries or personal data. Your privacy is protected by keeping all content on your device.

Permissions used by the app

  • Read External Storage Permission: Required only if you choose to add your own photos to the diary entries or app for personalization.
  • Set Wallpaper Permission: (If applicable) Used to set wallpapers or lock screen backgrounds when you use the app's wallpaper features.

Device Data

The app may collect information about the device you use to access the service, including:

  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers such as Android ID or Advertising ID
  • Application version and crash reports
  • Basic device settings and configuration

This data helps us fix bugs, improve app performance, and tailor the user experience.

Event and Usage Data

When you use the app, we collect aggregate usage data such as:

  • Which features are used most often
  • App session duration and screen views
  • Interaction events within the app

This data is collected anonymously and only to help us understand user behavior and improve the app experience.

IP Address

We collect your IP address when you connect to our services. This is used for:

  • Delivering ads to your device
  • Analytics and troubleshooting
  • Determining approximate location for statistical purposes only

We do not collect or use your precise location data.

Email Address

We collect your email address only if you contact us for support or feedback via email. Your email will be used solely to respond to your inquiries and provide support. We do not use or store your email for marketing or other purposes.

Analytics

The application does not collect any usage or crash reporting data without your explicit consent.

If you agree to data collection, we use analytics services to collect data essential to:

  • Understand how you use the app
  • Improve functionality and features
  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes

These analytics collect information such as anonymized device identifiers, app usage events, and anonymized IP addresses.

Advertising ID

The Advertising ID is used by our ad providers to show relevant ads based on your interests. Please read the privacy policies of our ad providers to understand how they handle your data:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Audience Network Privacy Policy

Personal Data Collection

The app does not collect any personal data that directly identifies you such as your name, email, or any information you type into the diary.

Third-Party Services Used

We use the following third-party services strictly to help us operate and improve the app:

  • Google AdMob: To display ads and generate revenue. AdMob may collect device data and advertising identifiers.
  • Facebook Audience Network: To display ads targeted to your interests.

These third parties are only permitted to use your data to support their advertising services and not for any other purpose.

Advertising

Ads help keep the app free. The free version uses:

  • Google AdMob
  • Facebook Audience Network

These networks may use cookies and behavioral retargeting to show you personalized ads both inside and outside the app.
