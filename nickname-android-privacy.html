Privacy Policy

This app is a nickname maker that generates creative and cool-looking words for gaming or online usernames. Please be advised that some combinations may include offensive words. If you are easily offended, we recommend not using this app.

Information We Collect

1. Local Data Only

The app does not require or offer account creation. All entries and generated nicknames remain on your device only. We do not collect or store personal data like names, emails, or text you input.

2. Device Data

We collect information such as device model, name, identifiers, crash info, app identifier, and OS settings. On our website, we also gather browser and system settings data. This helps us fix bugs and improve app performance.

3. Event and Usage Data

We track aggregate data about your interaction with the app or website (e.g., visited tabs or pages). This helps us understand which features are most useful and communicate timely content and updates.

4. IP Address

We collect your IP address to deliver services, determine approximate location (not precise GPS), and for regulatory compliance. This data is used for statistical purposes.

5. Support Requests

If you contact us for support, we receive your email address, which we use only to respond to your request.
