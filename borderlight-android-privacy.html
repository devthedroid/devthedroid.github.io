Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy statement explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by our application ("the Application").

Information Collection and Use

Since it's a wallpaper app, it creates live or image wallpapers and sets them to your device’s home screen when you click the "Set Wallpaper" button.

The Application does not offer account creation, so we do not store or access any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device.

Permissions Used

  • Read External Storage – Required if you want to add your own photo to set as wallpaper.
  • Set Wallpaper – Required to apply the wallpaper on your device.

Device Data

We collect device-related data such as device model, name, identifiers, settings, app ID, and crash logs. This helps us fix bugs and improve our services.

Event and Usage Data

We process data related to your usage of our services, such as which app screens you visit. This is aggregated to help improve functionality and user experience.

IP Address

We collect your IP address for statistical analysis, service delivery, and regulatory compliance. We do not collect precise location data.

Email Support

We collect your email only when you contact us for support, and only use it for communication regarding your query.

Analytics

We do not collect crash or usage data without your consent. If consent is given, we use analytics to improve our app, such as which features are most used.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Google Analytics & Firebase – Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Analytics – Privacy Policy

These services help us collect anonymized usage and crash data to improve the app.

Advertising

Advertising helps keep our app free. We use:

  • AdMob – Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Audience Network – Privacy Policy

They may use cookies or behavioral retargeting to show ads based on your interests. You can opt-out using your device settings.

Data Retention

Firebase retains crash stack traces for 180 days. Instance IDs are kept until deleted by request.

Security
