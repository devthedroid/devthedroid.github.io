Privacy Policy for My Personal Diary with Lock (iOS)

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use My Personal Diary with Lock ("we", "our", "us", or "the App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Diary Entries

  • Your diary entries are stored locally on your iOS device.
  • We do not upload, store, or access your diary content on any external server.
  • No cloud backup or synchronization is currently provided.

b. Personal Information

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • All data entered in the app remains private and local unless you interact with third-party services like ad networks or analytics.

c. Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how users interact with the App:

  • Anonymous usage data (e.g., screen views, session duration, crash reports)
  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version)
  • No personally identifiable information is collected.

d. Advertising Data

We use third-party ad networks to display ads:

  • Google AdMob
  • Meta Audience Network

These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Ad interactions and app usage data
  • General location (e.g., city-level)

You can manage your ad preferences via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt and your device settings.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Improve performance and fix bugs
  • Serve relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or trade your diary entries or personal data to any third parties.

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The App offers both one-time and subscription-based purchases. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions securely.

a. One-Time Purchase (Lifetime Premium)

  • A single purchase provides lifetime access to premium features.
  • No recurring charges.

b. Monthly and Yearly Subscriptions

  • Auto-renew every month or year unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal period.
  • Charged to your Apple ID account through Apple’s In-App Purchase system.

c. 7-Day Free Trial

  • New users can start with a 7-day free trial for subscription plans.
  • No charges apply during the free trial period.
  • To avoid being charged, you must cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the end of the trial period.

Note: You can manage or cancel your subscriptions in your App Store account settings at any time. We do not store or access your payment information.

4. Data Security

  • Diary data is stored only on your device.
  • The app includes an optional lock feature (PIN/password/Face ID) for additional protection.
  • Firebase, RevenueCat, and other services use encryption and security protocols to protect transmitted data.

However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet or mobile platform is 100% secure.
